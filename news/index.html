Y Design Studio Celebrating three years


21 Jan 2019
Celebrating three years in business is quite an achievement and what a journey it’s been… From a back bedroom to the lovely studio in Daniel Mews (via St James Parade), we’ve designed many brands for clients in Bath and beyond. On 9 September, we held the first of our birthday parties at the beautiful Holburne Museum. Over the years, many of our clients have become our friends, so this was our chance to celebrate together. Over 80 guests joined us to drink champagne, laugh a lot and listen to live jazz from the Tom Berge Trio. We had a fantastic evening and would like to thank everyone who came along.
On 20 January, we celebrated three years of design at the Huntsman with friends and colleagues, old and new. Thank you to all the talented people we’ve worked and collaborated with over the years.
